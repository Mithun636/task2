ABOUT US

MCS an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company headquartered in Mumbai. It is a part of the MCS Group and operates in 4 locations across 4 countries. In March 2024, it was reported that MCS had more than 601,546 employees worldwide. MCS is the second-largest Indian company by market capitalization, the most valuable IT service brands worldwide, and the top Big Tech (India) company.In 2012, it was the world's second-largest user of U.S. H-1B visas.
